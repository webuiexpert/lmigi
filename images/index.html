Complete Containment Solutions

LMI delivers integrated earthworks, geosynthetic, and haulage solutions for diverse clients across Australia. Backed by strong foundations, we have achieved outstanding results across multiple projects and industry sectors. We have the expertise to deliver complete solutions and the drive to ensure high-quality outcomes every time.


LMI was established in 2016 as a privately owned earthworks and lining contractor. Since the outset, we have gone above and beyond to create value for our clients. We specialise in the safe, efficient, and successful delivery of civil and environmental engineering, geosynthetic material supply and deployment, and haulage services.

At LMI, strong professional relationships and measured growth are the twin foundations of our business. We have grown in alignment with our values – finding innovative solutions and delivering project outcomes of increased complexity and value for our partners.

Our reputation is built on results. We are trusted to deliver complete solutions.
