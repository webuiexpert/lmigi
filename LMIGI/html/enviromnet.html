Who We Are

At LMI we take pride in our ability to provide a total solution, either as a contractor for both the earthworks and geosynthetic installation, or as a sub-contractor for the supply and installation of geosynthetics.​

Building a brighter
World
together.

Our capacity to deliver both the civil and geosynthetic functions for containment systems across industries such as water, waste, mining, oil and gas and agriculture sets us apart. LMIs civil earthworks expertise, combined with our knowledge of the geosynthetic industry, allows us to develop a ‘best for project’ approach for all our clients. ​

We employee highly skilled and qualified managers, supervisors, engineers, machine operators and geosynthetic installers. The unified management of LMI’s civil and geosynthetic supervisors under one construction manager ensure projects are completed to the highest quality, safely, on time and to budget.​
